Selecting the input device pt. 2

In this window you can also change the amount of audio samples that get analyzed, and increase the volume threshold of the input device.

Increasing the amount of audio samples will improve the audio analysis, but may also impact performance if it's set too high.

Increasing the volume threshold is a good idea when your microphone is picking up too much background noise, and you see the indicator jumping around frantically.
Be careful not to set it too high, though, because then your microphone might have trouble picking up any sound at all.
