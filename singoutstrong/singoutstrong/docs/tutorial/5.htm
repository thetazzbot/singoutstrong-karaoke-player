Get more songs!

Ok, so you'd probably like to play something other then Jingle Bells at some point.
This player supports the popular UltraStar and MIDI song formats.

To find more songs just open the Song Search window.
Type in the search term and specify whether you're looking for the song title, artist, or both, and select the format you're looking for.

The results will be displayed in the table, and if you click on one of them, you'll be taken a website where you can download the song.
