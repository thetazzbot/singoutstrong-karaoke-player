Ok, so you'd probably like to play something other then Jingle Bells at some point.
This player supports the popular UltraStar format, and the ubiquitous midi format.

To find your favorite song in the UltraStar format you can check out:

  • The USDB website (you'll need your own mp3, though)
  • or just Google 'SONG_TITLE UltraStar'

Or to find it in the midi format:

  • VanBasco has a midi file search engine
  • or again, just Google 'SONG_TITLE midi'
